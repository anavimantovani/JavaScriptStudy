What is this website?

This is the Web site for Ms. Sure's 5th Grade Science Class.

Why?

This Web site is designed to calculate your grade, check the measurements from a previous activity and give feedback to our teacher!

Who is Ms. Sure?

Ms. Sure has been teaching science for more than 15 years! She's the only teacher at Sterling Middle School who has caught herself on fire during lab. She enjoys hiking and biking, and her favorite place to visit is Yosemite National Park. Her favorite TV show is Mythbusters.

Discover the wonders of nature with us and learn new things along the way!
